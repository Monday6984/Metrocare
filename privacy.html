Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

We collect only what we need to deliver safe, quality support — and we protect it accordingly.

Information we collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide NDIS supports, including contact details, NDIS plan information, health and support needs, and communication preferences — generally provided directly by participants, families or referring coordinators.

How we use it

Information is used to assess suitability for services, deliver and coordinate support, meet NDIS reporting obligations, and communicate with participants, families and support coordinators.

How we protect it

We store information securely, limit access to staff who need it to do their jobs, and never sell personal information to third parties.

Your rights

You can request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask questions about how it's used at any time.

Contact us about privacy

For any privacy-related question or request, contact us at info@metrocareservices.com.au or call 08 8102 2398.
